No Network Requests The extension never makes any network requests. It works entirely offline after installation.

Full Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

Dark Mode Pro does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. The extension operates entirely within your browser.

2. Local Storage

Your preferences (theme, brightness, per-site settings, schedule) are stored using Chrome's chrome.storage.sync API, which syncs across your Chrome profile. This data never leaves Google's infrastructure and is not accessible to us.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No external scripts are loaded.

5. Children's Privacy

Our extension does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. No data is collected from any user.

6. Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be reflected in the Chrome Web Store listing and in the extension's changelog.
